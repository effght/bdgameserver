Current Features

Built for motion-controlled badminton practice and playful rally experiments.

Mobile Swing Input

Your phone sends swing timing, face angle, ready prompts, and special-shot commands directly to the court view.

Rally Simulation

Rally mode includes scoring, serving flow, stamina, shot stats, landing maps, AI positioning, and controller-ready prompts.

AI Personalities

Different AI styles can bias shot choice, movement, front-court behavior, and special-skill usage.

Special Skills

Optional skills include power shots, redirects, time effects, invisible shots, forced responses, countdown states, and more.

Pairing Flow

Pairing is designed to be simple: open the court, scan the code, calibrate, and play.

1. Open the player court

Go to /player. The page creates or restores a room id and shows a QR code for the controller.

2. Scan the QR code

Your phone opens /hit with the same room id and a WebSocket URL pointing to /ws.

3. Calibrate and play

Enable sensors, calibrate the phone face, connect the controller, then start swinging or use rally-ready prompts.
